Product Pulse: User Feedback Summary (November 17 - November 23, 2025)

Hi Team,

Here's a quick product pulse, summarizing user feedback collected between November 17 and November 23, 2025, 2025. This review helps us understand user sentiment and identify areas for improvement within our platform.

*   **Overview:** This weekly pulse summarizes user feedback from November 17th to 23rd, 2025. Key areas of concern include app performance issues, high and unexpected charges, and feature requests. Addressing these issues can improve user satisfaction and retention.

*   **Top Themes:**
    *   App Glitches & Errors: Negative sentiment surrounds app stability, with users reporting login problems, unresponsiveness, and slow performance impacting trading experience.
    *   High Charges & Hidden Fees: Users express strong dissatisfaction with high brokerage fees and unexpected charges, impacting profitability and perceived value compared to competitors.
    *   Feature Requests & Improvements: While users find the app beginner-friendly, there's a desire for enhanced stock picking assistance and improvements to existing features.

*   **Key Quotes:**
    *   "[App Glitches & Errors] Always lags on price reflection, orders, which is the basic requirement."
    *   "[High Charges & Hidden Fees] Very high brokerage and hidden charges... in 7k profit I got only 940 rs."
    *   "[Feature Requests & Improvements] It doesn't help in picking the right stock."

*   **Action Ideas:**
    *   [App Glitches & Errors] Prioritize investigation and resolution of reported app glitches and performance issues to improve user experience.
    *   [High Charges & Hidden Fees] Review fee structure and disclosures to ensure transparency and competitiveness.
    *   [Feature Requests & Improvements] Explore and prioritize feature enhancements related to stock picking assistance based on user feedback.
